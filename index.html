Guangshun Wei 魏广顺

Ph.D., Researcher

guangshunwei@gmail.com

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Hong Kong. I received his B.Sc. degree in computer science and Technology from College of Information Science and Engineering, University of Jinan, in 2017. I received a Ph.D. from the School of Software, Shandong University, Jinan, China, in 2022. My current research interests include machine learning, image processing, and geometric analysis.
