Associate Research Fellow · School of Software, Shandong University

山东大学学无止境 · 气有浩然

I am an Associate Research Fellow at the School of Software, Shandong University. From May 2023 to July 2024, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong. I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology from the University of Jinan in 2017, and my Ph.D. degree from Shandong University in 2022.

My research focuses on 3D geometric processing and reconstruction, intelligent graphics and image processing, and multimodal dental/medical data processing. I am particularly interested in geometry-aware learning, point cloud analysis, image-conditioned 3D generation, digital orthodontics, and reliable AI systems for medical data.
